Cast

Jack Dawson Played byLeonardo DiCaprio

Jack Dawson is a free-spirited, struggling artist with a passion for adventure. He wins a third-class ticket to the Titanic in a lucky game of poker. Onboard, he meets Rose, saving her from a desperate situation and introducing her to a world of freedom and love. Jack's presence brings a profound change to her life as they embark on a romantic and transformative journey together.

Rose DeWitt Bukater Played byKate Winslet

Rose DeWitt Bukater is a 17-year-old from a prestigious family, trapped in an unhappy engagement to a wealthy but controlling fiancé. Struggling with the constraints of her upper-class life, she finds a kindred spirit in Jack. Through their passionate romance, Rose discovers a new perspective on life and the courage to seek her own destiny.

Caledon "Cal" Hockley Played byBilly Zane

Cal Hockley, Rose's affluent and arrogant fiancé, represents the opulence and rigidity of the upper class. His possessive nature and jealousy of Jack fuel the dramatic tension on the Titanic. Cal's desperate attempts to maintain control during the ship's disaster reveal his true character and the lengths he will go to survive.

Ruth DeWitt Bukater Played byFrances Fisher

Ruth DeWitt Bukater, Rose's traditional and status-conscious mother, pressures her daughter into the engagement with Cal to secure their financial future. Ruth's relentless pursuit of social standing often puts her at odds with Rose's desire for independence and love.

Molly Brown Played byKathy Bates

Molly Brown, a self-made woman with a generous heart, stands out among the first-class passengers for her down-to-earth demeanor and compassion. She befriends Jack and Rose, offering support and practical wisdom. During the catastrophe, Molly's bravery and leadership shine as she fights to save others.

Thomas Andrews Played byThomas Andrews

Thomas Andrews, the ship's dedicated designer, embodies the blend of pride and tragedy associated with the Titanic. His commitment to the safety of the passengers is unwavering, and he works tirelessly to help them during the ship's final hours. Andrews’ poignant awareness of the unfolding disaster underscores the human element of the Titanic’s legacy.

Feel the heart-wrenching drama and timeless romance of "Titanic" through the eyes of these unforgettable characters. Their stories of love, sacrifice, and survival continue to resonate with audiences around the world.
